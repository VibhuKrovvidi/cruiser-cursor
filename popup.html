Cruiser Cursor

Be smart, move your wrist less

Cruiser Cursor allows users to avoid moving their wrists by providing them a smart cursor.

Simply begin moving your mouse and Cruiser Cursor will try to find the most relevant clickable object in that direction.

Then, with a simple gesture, you can choose to click, saving you time and energy as you browse the web!
